About Me & The Mission

Hi, I'm Shashi Vadhan Reddy

I'm a student and entrepreneur from Telangana, India on a mission to make the digital world safer. While balancing a full school day in Class 9, I taught myself about cybersecurity and saw how smaller businesses often lack the resources to check their online security.

That's how White Crocodile AI began—a proud Indian startup and B2B tool I built from the ground up that automates website vulnerability scanning to give businesses clear, actionable reports. My vision is to provide enterprise-level security insights that are accessible, affordable, and easy to understand.

I believe the best solutions come from curiosity and a willingness to start, no matter your age. This Made in India tool is just the beginning of building a more secure internet for everyone, starting from our great nation.
